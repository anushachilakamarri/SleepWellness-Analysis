# **Sleep Wellness Analysis Dashboard**

## **The Business Problem**
In todayâ€™s fast-paced environment, sleep quality and stress levels directly impact health and productivity. Organizations and individuals often lack the tools to analyze multi-factor wellness data, which makes it difficult to identify improvement areas.

## **Key Insights and Impact**
- Engineers and doctors show higher average sleeping hours compared to sales professionals.
- Higher stress levels are correlated with lower sleep duration.
- Obese individuals have higher average heart rates compared to other BMI categories.
- Daily physical activity shows a positive relationship with sleep quality, especially in females.
- The dashboard reveals which professions are at highest risk for poor sleep and stress, guiding targeted wellness initiatives.
